Clubcard

An exciting new offer is available from May 2011 for our customers to apply for a Bhat-Bhateni Club Card, “a loyalty card” of Bhat-Bhateni Super Market. You can apply for a club card at any Bhat-Bhateni Stores or visit our website to download application forms. All purchases on standard priced products that total Rs. 500 or more will accumulate points. For every 500 rupees spent, 1 point is awarded which is equivalent to at least 6.5 rupees of shopping vouchers. These vouchers will be awarded at the end of each quarter if more than 200 points have been accumulated. Additionally, surprise gifts may be sent on special occasions. The Bhat-Bhateni club card will be supported by a dedicated customer service team and the point balance will be available on the receipt. You can complete the application form and submit it to the nearest Bhatbhateni Store. Please refer to our terms and conditions and FAQs for more details. Gift vouchers of Rs. 100, Rs. 500 and Rs. 1000 can also be purchased at the Bhat-Bhateni Head Office, Tangal, and Maharajgunj stores. Please contact Ms. Pramila Rai to purchase gift vouchers.
